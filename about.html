About the MVNP Research Repository

The Mahagnao Volcano Natural Park (MVNP) Research Repository serves as the centralized archive of scientific studies, technical reports, and research outputs that support evidence-based management of the protected area (PA).

What is the MVNP Research Repository?

The MVNP Research Repository is an internal knowledge platform maintained by the Protected Area Management Office (PAMO) of Mahagnao Volcano Natural Park. It consolidates research clearances, final reports, datasets, maps, and documentation submitted by researchers, partner institutions, and government agencies conducting work within the protected area.

It preserves institutional memory, prevents duplication, and ensures that decisions for the protected area are grounded in science.

Objectives

  • Centralize all research outputs relevant to Mahagnao Volcano Natural Park.
  • Support evidence-based planning, monitoring, and decision-making within the protected area.
  • Provide reference materials for researchers, SUCs, LGUs, and stakeholders.
  • Ensure compliance with the MVNP Research Guidelines and environmental laws.

Governance and Management

The repository is managed by the Protected Area Management Office (PAMO), with oversight from the MVNP Protected Area Management Board (MVNP-PAMB). Research proposals and outputs undergo technical review by the Research Technical Working Group (TWG).

The PAMO archives and catalogues submissions, while the PAMB ensures alignment with Protected Area governance and the Management Plan.

Scope of the Repository

  • Non-invasive studies (biodiversity monitoring, ecological surveys, assessments).
  • Sampling-based studies with appropriate permits and clearances.
  • Published journal articles, undergraduate theses, and technical reports.
  • Legacy studies conducted before the MVNP Research Guidelines.

Sensitive information, such as exact coordinates of threatened species, may be restricted and require coordination with the PAMO.

Use of Repository Information

Repository content is intended to guide Protected Area (PA) management, conservation planning, and policymaking. Users are encouraged to properly acknowledge the original authors and MVNP.

For datasets or materials not directly downloadable, coordination with the PAMO is required.

Legal and Policy Basis

  • Republic Act No. 11038 – ENIPAS Act of 2018
  • Republic Act No. 9147 – Wildlife Resources Conservation and Protection Act
  • MVNP Research Guidelines on the Engagement / Conduct of Scientific Research and Studies
  • Relevant DENR Administrative Orders and technical bulletins
